Tree Plantation Drive

Growing a Greener and Healthier Future

28 December 2025 | Environmental Initiative

Our NGO conducted a Tree Plantation Drive with the mission of protecting the environment and spreading awareness about the importance of trees in maintaining ecological balance.

Volunteers enthusiastically participated by planting saplings in public spaces, school premises, and roadside areas. The drive aimed to promote oxygen-rich surroundings, reduce pollution, and encourage community involvement in environmental conservation.

“Plant a tree today for a greener tomorrow.”

The event concluded with a pledge to nurture and protect the planted saplings and continue similar initiatives in the future.
