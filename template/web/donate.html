FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donations

Yes! Samajseva Foundation is registered under 80G of Income Tax Act. All donations are eligible for 50% tax deduction. You will receive an 80G certificate via email within 24 hours of your donation.

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and Bank Transfers (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS). International donors can use PayPal or wire transfer.

85% of your donation goes directly to our programs and beneficiaries. 10% is used for administrative costs and 5% for fundraising. We maintain complete transparency and publish annual reports with detailed fund utilization.

Yes, you can make a tribute donation in honor or memory of someone special. During the donation process, you can add dedication details and we can send a notification to the honoree's family if you wish.

We welcome volunteers! You can fill out the contact form or email us at volunteer@samajseva.org with your details and areas of interest. We have opportunities for both on-ground and remote volunteering.
