Who Can Volunteer?

There is no perfect volunteer. Anyone with a heart to serve can join.

  • Age: 18+ years old
  • Students: Looking for internships/experience
  • Professionals: Wanting to give back on weekends
  • Homemakers: With free time and a kind heart
